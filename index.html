Project 1

This project was designed to work as a fully functional calender, with a day, week, and month view. It also generates random events from the user-specified category to offer ideas for what to do when you have no clue what could make your boredom go away!

Work Day Scheduler

This project featured local storage, and was designed to dislay an 9 hr day and have events saveable to the users local system. Then, the user could return to their plans later to check off or intiate new future plans.

My Car Game

This simple car game features the ability to honk, drive to work and around world, and get a tune up for your car. If, Else statements make it possible to restrict user inputs to certain letters and prerequisites.
